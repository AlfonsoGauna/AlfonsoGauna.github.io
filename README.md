# Alfonso Gauna

<img src="images/headshot.jpeg" alt="Alfonso Gauna" width="220">

I am a PhD student in the Kenneth C. Griffin Department of Economics at the University of Chicago. I hold both a BA and an MA in Economics from Universidad Torcuato di Tella.

My research interests lie at the intersection of macroeconomic theory, finance, and international economics. I have been working on topics related to small open economies, the role of expectations in macroeconomics, labor issues, and sovereign debt in Latin American countries. To learn more about my research, visit my [research page](https://alfonsogauna.com/research.html).
